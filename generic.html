Our Growing Practices

No-till: Improves nutrient cycling, reduces erosion and increases the soil's ability to absorb and retain water.

Crop Rotation & Intercropping: Monocultural systems are highly susceptible to pests and disease. We plant to maximize biodiversity.

Natural Pest Control: We utilize physical barriers, natural predators and cultural controls to avoid any use of chemical intervention.

Rotating Livestock: Our hens freely graze our farm providing natural fertility, pest control and weed management.
